Color Blocking Tool

Color Pal

It is an iPad app helping illustrators or artists to choose and apply colors to their digital artwork. It guides them in doing color blocking based on some inspiration and objectives that users have in mind. Ultimately it reduces the burden of time-consuming on working and fosters efficiency in their work.
